Hi, we are the team behind Bankster Web.

We are a group of Computer Engineering students who are always enthusiastic to learn and try to make the best website possible. This semester, we were assigned to develop a web application, and this is the result.

So, what is Bankster Web? Simply put, it's a tool to help you find ATM locations and branches of various banks across Indonesia quickly and easily, without the hassle of opening multiple apps.

Bank BCA (Bank Central Asia)

PT Bank Central Asia Tbk (BCA) is one of the largest private banks in Indonesia, established in 1957. The bank is widely recognized for its focus on transaction banking services, supported by an extensive network of ATMs and branches, as well as strong digital innovation through mobile and internet banking services. BCA serves a diverse range of customer segments, from individuals, MSMEs, to corporations, and has consistently demonstrated solid financial performance.

Bank BSI (Bank Syariah Indonesia)

PT Bank Syariah Indonesia Tbk (BSI) is the largest Islamic bank in Indonesia which officially operates on February 1, 2021. BSI is the result of the merger of three state-owned Islamic banks, namely PT Bank Syariah Mandiri, PT Bank BNI Syariah, and PT Bank BRI Syariah. As the largest Islamic banking entity, BSI operates based on Islamic sharia principles and aims to be the main driving force in the development of the halal industry ecosystem as well as serving the Islamic financial needs of the Indonesian people at large.

Bank BJB (Bank Pembangunan Daerah Jawa Barat dan Banten)

PT Bank Pembangunan Daerah Jawa Barat dan Banten Tbk (Bank BJB) is a regional development bank (BPD) owned by the West Java Provincial Government, Banten Provincial Government, as well as a number of city/regency governments in both provinces. Bank BJB has a strategic role in supporting regional economic development, especially in West Java and Banten, by providing banking services to local governments, Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), corporations, and the general public. The bank is also active in financing regional infrastructure projects.
